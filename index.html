About

Translating AI potential
into measurable impact

My career has been defined by one consistent challenge: taking complex, emerging technology and translating it into something field teams can sell, buyers can trust, and organizations can adopt at scale.

Today that work centers on agentic AI — building the frameworks, teams, and GTM motions that move enterprises from experimentation to measurable adoption. I believe the gap between AI potential and AI impact is almost never technical. It's organizational.

I also write and speak about what it means to work effectively in an AI-accelerated world — not as an AI expert, but as someone who has watched what actually changes when it enters a business.
